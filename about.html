Me, as a designer

Good design is mostly subtraction.

Fewer steps, fewer surprises, fewer calls to support. That’s the thread through my work — from a paper-bound claims pipeline to a CRM five teams share to a marketplace that compresses weeks of negotiation into a tap. I like problems where the workflow itself is the product, and where the answer starts with watching people work.

As the first in-house designer at Veteran Benefits Guide, I set the UX direction across consumer, internal, and enterprise surfaces — and built the design system that holds them together.
